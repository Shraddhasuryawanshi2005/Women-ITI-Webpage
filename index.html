ABOUT OUR I.T.I

Our Industrial Training Institute (ITI) is committed to delivering quality vocational education and hands-on training to students across various trades. With a mission to skill the youth and prepare them for the modern workforce , our ITI offers a range of technical courses such as Electrician , COPA , Stenography , Instrument Mechanic .

TRADE

COMPUTER OPERATOR AND PROGRAMMING ASSISTENT

COPA is a popular ITI Trade designed to provide students with fundamental knowledge and practical skilles in the field of computer operations and programming . It is a 1-year full-time course offered by Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) under the NCVT( National Council For Vocational Traning ).

STENO GRAPHY HINDI AND ENGLISH

STENOGRAPHY is a professional Trade that trains students in shorthand writing and typing skills in both Hindi and English . It is widely used in courts, government offices ,media , and corporate sectors for fast and accurate documentation of spoken words.

INSTRUMENT MECHANIC

Instrument Mechanic trade is a technical course in ITI that trains students to maintain, repair, and calibrate precision instruments used in various industries like oil, gas, pharmaceuticals, and power plants.

ELECTRICIAN

Electrician Trade is a vocational course that trains students in electrical wiring, systems, and equipment. It's one of the most popular trades in ITIs across India due to high demand in industries and households for electrical maintenance and installation.

FACILITIES

MODERN CLASSROOM

A modern classroom in an ITI (Industrial Training Institute) incorporates technology and flexible learning spaces to enhance the learning experience. These classrooms often feature multimedia tools, digital learning platforms, and adaptive learning software, alongside a flexible design that accommodates both individual and group work.

PRACTICAL LAB

Practical labs are essential for hands-on training in various trades. These labs provide students with the opportunity to apply theoretical knowledge gained in the classroom to real-world scenarios, enhancing their skills and preparing them for industry demands. They are equipped with tools, machines, and materials relevant to specific trades like COPA,electrician, fitter, welder, and more.

WORKSHOP

Workshops are practical training areas equipped with tools and machinery relevant to specific trades. They provide hands-on experience for students to develop skills and knowledge necessary for various technical professions. These workshops are designed to simulate real-world industrial settings, enabling students to gain practical experience in areas like machining, electrical work, or welding.

VEHICLE PARKING

ITIs typically allocate parking spaces, which may include areas within the campus or designated parking lots .
